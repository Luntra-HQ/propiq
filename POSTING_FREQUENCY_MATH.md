### Business Metrics (Track These):
- ‚úÖ Website visits from social
- ‚úÖ Email sign-ups from social
- ‚úÖ Trial sign-ups from social
- ‚úÖ Paid conversions from social
- ‚úÖ Cost per acquisition (time √∑ conversions)

**Formula:**
```
Social ROI = (Revenue from social - Time invested) √∑ Time invested

Example:

Month 12: $10,500 MRR

ROI: ($50,400 - $18,200) / $18,200 = +177% (profitable)
```

**Lesson:** First 6 months is investment, 6-12 months is payoff
