---

### Blocker #4: Pricing Structure Misalignment
**Problem:** Analysis limits create friction, not value.

**Investor Behavior:**
- Analyze 20-50 properties to buy ONE
- Want UNLIMITED exploration
- Analysis is a means to an end (finding the deal)

**Current Pricing Psychology:**
- "2 analyses left" = anxiety, not value
- Creates artificial scarcity on a digital good
- Encourages screenshots instead of subscription
- Feels like a trap, not a tool

**Better Model:** Unlimited analyses, tier by capabilities
- Free: Basic calculator only
- Starter ($49): Unlimited AI analysis, core metrics
- Pro ($99): Advanced insights, comps, market data
- Elite ($199): White-label, API access, deal alerts

---

### Blocker #5: No Trust/Social Proof
**Problem:** New platform, zero credibility.

**Missing Elements:**
- Customer testimonials ("Found my first rental with PropIQ!")
- Case studies (deal breakdowns, actual ROI)
- Track record (% of deals that performed vs PropIQ prediction)
- Social proof (users, analyses run, success stories)
- Authority (who built this? Real estate background?)
