## ðŸŽ¯ System Philosophy

**Goal:** Maximize signal, minimize noise, enable daily learning without overwhelm.

**Principles:**
1. **Quality > Quantity** - 10 great newsletters > 50 mediocre ones
2. **Actionable > Inspirational** - Tactics you can implement this week
3. **Ruthless Curation** - Unsubscribe fast, re-subscribe if you miss it
4. **Time-Boxed** - 5 min daily, 30 min weekly max
5. **Business-Aligned** - Every subscription should help PropIQ or your SaaS journey
