## Best Practices

**Hook (First 2 Lines):**
- Must stop the scroll
- Use bold statements, questions, or surprising facts
- Examples:
  - "The 1% rule will lose you money."
  - "After analyzing 500 properties, I learned this:"
  - "Most investors are doing this backwards."

**Body:**
- Use bullet points and emojis for scannability
- Keep paragraphs 1-2 lines max
- Add whitespace for readability

**CTA (Call to Action):**
- Ask a question to drive engagement
- Include link (use UTM codes)
- Make it clear what to do next

**Length:**
- Minimum: 1,200 characters
- Sweet spot: 1,300-1,800 characters
- Maximum: 3,000 characters

**Timing:**
- Best: Tuesday-Thursday 7-9 AM ET
- Good: Monday/Friday 12-1 PM ET
- Avoid: Weekends (lower B2B engagement)

## Content Creation Workflow

1. **Pick a template** based on your goal
2. **Choose a topic** from ideas bank
3. **Fill in the template** with your content
4. **Add UTM link** for tracking
5. **Select 5-7 hashtags** from the bank
6. **Save in appropriate folder** (educational, product, etc.)
7. **Schedule in Buffer** for optimal time
8. **Engage in first hour** after publishing
9. **Track metrics** after 24-48 hours
10. **Update content library** with performance data
