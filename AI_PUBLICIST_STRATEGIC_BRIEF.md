## üí° STRATEGIC PRINCIPLES

### Principle 1: PropIQ First
Every content decision should ask: "Does this help PropIQ?"
- Will this attract PropIQ's target users?
- Will this demonstrate PropIQ's value?
- Will this convert readers to users?

### Principle 2: Own Your Distribution
Build on platforms you control:
- Email list (100% owned)
- YouTube (searchable, discoverable)
- SEO blog (evergreen traffic)

Don't rely on:
- Social media algorithms (Instagram, TikTok, Twitter)
- Platforms that can delete you overnight

### Principle 3: Teach What You Use
Best content = Using PropIQ to solve real problems
- Live property analysis sessions
- Real market research examples
- Actual deals evaluated with PropIQ

### Principle 4: Build for Exits
Every content piece should:
1. Be evergreen (valuable in 6 months)
2. Include clear CTA (email signup or PropIQ trial)
3. Demonstrate expertise (build authority)

### Principle 5: Think Multi-Brand
Content infrastructure built for PropIQ can launch:
- PropIQ (real estate SaaS)
- Future Product B (different niche, same audience type)
- Future Product C (leverage same distribution)
