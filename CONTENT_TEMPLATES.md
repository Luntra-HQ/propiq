## Template Usage Guide

### Weekly Content Mix

**Monday:** Build Update (Template 1)
**Tuesday:** Hot Take (Template 4)
**Wednesday:** Lesson Learned (Template 2)
**Thursday:** Founder Journey (Template 3)
**Friday:** Value Bomb (Template 7)
**Saturday:** Problem Story (Template 8)
**Sunday:** Milestone or Customer Story (Template 5 or 6)

### Customization Checklist

Before posting, always:
- [ ] Replace [placeholders] with specific details
- [ ] Add real metrics (users, analyses, days)
- [ ] Include screenshot or visual if mentioned
- [ ] Update "Day X" with actual day count
- [ ] Replace [link] with actual PropIQ URL
- [ ] Add personal touch (your voice, your story)
- [ ] Proofread for typos and clarity
- [ ] Test character count for Twitter version

### Engagement Multipliers

**After posting:**
1. Comment on 10 posts from your target list (BEFORE posting)
2. Reply to every comment on your post within 1 hour
3. Quote tweet or share insights from engagement
4. DM interesting commenters with genuine value
5. Log all engagement in Daily Engagement Tracker
