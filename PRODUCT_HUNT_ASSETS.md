## 🎬 Product Demo Video

### Specifications
- **Length:** 30-60 seconds (max)
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264)
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080px (1080p)
- **File Size:** Under 100MB
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
- **Framerate:** 30fps
- **Audio:** Optional (most PH users watch muted)

### Script (30-second version)

```
[0-5s] HOOK
Screen: Messy spreadsheet with timer "30 minutes per property"
Voiceover/Text: "Analyzing properties takes forever..."

[5-10s] SOLUTION
Screen: PropIQ logo animation
Text: "PropIQ does it in 30 seconds"

[10-20s] DEMO
Screen: Fast walkthrough
- Enter address: "123 Main St, Austin TX"
- Click "Analyze"
- AI processing (show loading)
- Results appear: Cap Rate 6.2%, Cash Flow +$850, Score 82/100

[20-25s] BENEFIT
Screen: Multiple property cards
Text: "Analyze 20-50 properties to find THE ONE"

[25-30s] CTA
Screen: Pricing page or signup
Text: "Try free. No credit card required."
URL: propiq.luntra.one
```

### Production Notes
- Use screen recording (Loom, ScreenFlow, or OBS)
- Add text overlays (no voiceover needed)
- Keep pace fast (no wasted seconds)
- Use real product, real data
- Add subtle background music (optional)
- Include cursor movement (shows it's real)

### Tools
- **Recording:** Loom (https://www.loom.com)
- **Editing:** DaVinci Resolve (free) or Descript
- **Text Overlays:** Kapwing or Canva
- **Music:** Epidemic Sound or Artlist (royalty-free)

- High contrast for readability
- Real data over Lorem Ipsum
- Show, don't tell (use visuals)
- Mobile-first mindset
