## ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips from Your Mentor

**Building in Public:**
- Share the messy middle, not just wins
- Numbers are interesting (67 likes, 12 comments, 5 signups)
- Day count creates accountability (Day 47, Day 48...)

**Storytelling (Leandrew Robinson Style):**
- Start with a hook ("I just lost $50K...")
- Use specific details (not "a property" but "$600K Miami condo")
- End with a lesson (what you learned, how PropIQ helps)

**Value Bombs (Sahil Bloom Style):**
- Teach one complete framework
- Use numbered lists (7 metrics, 5 steps, 3 principles)
- Make it scannable (emojis, bullet points, white space)

**Consistency:**
- Post at same time daily (builds habit for your audience)
- Don't wait for perfection (done > perfect)
- Batch content (write 7 posts on Sunday, schedule for week)
