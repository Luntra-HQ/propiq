## Key Features (All v3.1.x)

### Core Capabilities
- ✅ **AI Property Analysis** - GPT-4o-mini powered property investment analysis
- ✅ **Deal Calculator** - 3-tab interface (Basic, Advanced, Scenarios)
- ✅ **Support Chat** - Custom AI support assistant
- ✅ **PDF Export** - Professional analysis reports

### Investment Strategies
- ✅ Traditional Rental
- ✅ House Hack (multi-unit)
- ✅ BRRRR Strategy
- ✅ Fix & Flip
- ✅ Commercial

### Property Types
- ✅ Single Family Home
- ✅ Multi-Family (2-4 units)
- ✅ Condo
- ✅ Townhouse
- ✅ Apartment Building (5+ units)
- ✅ Commercial

### Financial Metrics
- ✅ Deal Score (0-100)
- ✅ Monthly/Annual Cash Flow
- ✅ Cap Rate, Cash-on-Cash Return
- ✅ 1% Rule, DSCR, GRM
- ✅ 5-Year Projections
- ✅ Scenario Analysis (Best/Realistic/Worst)

### Expenses Supported
- ✅ Property Tax, Insurance
- ✅ **HOA Fees** (added in v3.1.1)
- ✅ Utilities, Maintenance
- ✅ Vacancy Reserve
- ✅ Property Management
