## üéØ Product Overview

### What is PropIQ?

**PropIQ** is an AI-powered real estate investment analysis platform that helps investors make data-driven decisions in seconds, not hours.

**Core Value Proposition:**
> "Analyze any property in 30 seconds. AI-powered insights that used to take hours of spreadsheet work."

### Key Features

1. **AI Property Analysis**
   - Enter any address ‚Üí Get comprehensive investment analysis
   - GPT-4o-mini powered insights
   - Market trends, financials, risk assessment
   - Pros/cons breakdown with actionable next steps

2. **Deal Calculator**
   - 3-tab interface (Basic, Advanced, Scenarios)
   - Real-time financial calculations
   - Deal scoring (0-100)
   - 5-year projections
   - Multiple investment strategies supported

3. **PropIQ AI Support Chat**
   - Custom AI assistant (saves $888/year vs Intercom)
   - Real-time property investment questions
   - Context-aware responses

4. **PDF Export**
   - Professional analysis reports
   - Share with partners/lenders
   - LinkedIn interview demo feature

### Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Price | Analyses/Month | Target Audience |

### Common Questions & Answers

**Q: How accurate is the AI analysis?**
A: PropIQ uses GPT-4o-mini and pulls from thousands of data points. We recommend using it as a starting point‚Äîalways verify with local market data and due diligence. Our AI is trained on real investment principles, but nothing replaces boots-on-the-ground research.

**Q: Can I export my analysis?**
A: Yes! Click the "Export to PDF" button on any analysis. You can share it with partners, lenders, or your team.

**Q: What if I want to analyze more than my tier allows?**
A: You can upgrade anytime, or purchase one-time top-up packs. Go to Settings ‚Üí Billing to see options.

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**
A: Yes, 30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. Just email brian@luntra.one.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Absolutely. No contracts, no commitments. Cancel from Settings ‚Üí Billing or email us.

**Q: Do you offer team/enterprise plans?**
A: Not yet, but it's coming! Email brian@luntra.one to join the waitlist for team features.
