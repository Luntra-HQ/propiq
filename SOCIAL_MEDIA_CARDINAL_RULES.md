## üö® Universal Cardinal Rules (All Platforms)

### 1. **Post Natively**
- ‚ùå Don't use third-party schedulers for first post
- ‚úÖ Post directly in the app for maximum reach
- üì± Apps can detect non-native posts and suppress them

### 2. **Timing Matters**
- ‚è∞ Post when your audience is most active
- üìä Check analytics for your best times
- üîÑ Don't post multiple times in quick succession (space out 4-6 hours)

- üî• Algorithm boosts posts with early engagement

### 4. **Quality Over Quantity**

- üéØ Better to post 3x/week with quality than daily with low effort

- üé® Vertical video for TikTok/Reels, professional for LinkedIn

## üì∏ Instagram Cardinal Rules

### Reels (Most Important)

**DO:**
- ‚úÖ Post reels directly in the Reels tab
- ‚úÖ Use trending audio (check "trending" in audio library)
- ‚úÖ Hook in first 1-3 seconds
- ‚úÖ Vertical 9:16 format (1080x1920px)
- ‚úÖ Keep under 90 seconds (30-60 is optimal)
- ‚úÖ Add captions (80% watch without sound)
- ‚úÖ Use 3-5 relevant hashtags in caption
- ‚úÖ Post at 9am, 12pm, or 6pm EST (peak times)

**DON'T:**
- ‚ùå **Don't share reel to your Story immediately** (kills reach)
- ‚ùå Don't repost TikToks with watermarks
- ‚ùå Don't use copyrighted music (use Instagram's library)
- ‚ùå Don't add too many hashtags (looks spammy, max 5)
- ‚ùå Don't use low-resolution video
- ‚ùå Don't add borders or letterboxing (looks recycled)

**Timing:**
- Wait 24-48 hours BEFORE sharing reel to your story
- Post new reels 24+ hours apart

### Posts (Photo/Carousel)

**DO:**
- ‚úÖ Use carousel posts (10 slides max) - 2x engagement
- ‚úÖ High-quality images (1080x1080px minimum)
- ‚úÖ Write meaningful captions (1500-2000 characters is optimal)
- ‚úÖ Ask questions to drive comments
- ‚úÖ Use first comment for hashtags (keeps caption clean)
- ‚úÖ Tag relevant accounts (but not too many)

**DON'T:**
- ‚ùå Don't use more than 10-15 hashtags
- ‚ùå Don't post low-quality or blurry images
- ‚ùå Don't use banned hashtags (check: https://banned.hashtags.org)
- ‚ùå Don't edit post after publishing (kills reach)

### Stories

**DO:**
- ‚úÖ Post 3-7 stories per day (consistency matters)
- ‚úÖ Use interactive stickers (polls, questions, quizzes)
- ‚úÖ Reply to all DMs from stories
- ‚úÖ Use text overlay (readable without sound)
- ‚úÖ Mix personal + business content

**DON'T:**
- ‚ùå Don't share new reels to story immediately (wait 24-48 hours)
- ‚ùå Don't post more than 10 stories in a row (split them up)
- ‚ùå Don't ignore story replies

## üéµ TikTok Cardinal Rules

### General

**DO:**
- ‚úÖ Use trending sounds (check "For You" page)
- ‚úÖ Hook in first 1 second (literally)
- ‚úÖ Vertical format only (9:16)
- ‚úÖ Native captions (auto-generate, then edit)
- ‚úÖ Jump cuts every 2-3 seconds (keeps attention)
- ‚úÖ 15-30 seconds is optimal (max 60)
- ‚úÖ Post 1-3x per day (TikTok rewards consistency)
- ‚úÖ Engage with comments immediately

**DON'T:**
- ‚ùå Don't post Instagram watermarks (instant suppression)
- ‚ùå Don't add link stickers (TikTok hates external traffic)
- ‚ùå Don't use copyrighted audio from outside TikTok
- ‚ùå Don't post horizontal videos
- ‚ùå Don't ask for follows/likes in video (against TOS)
- ‚ùå Don't use more than 3-5 hashtags

**Timing:**
- Post at 6am, 10am, or 7pm EST
- Watch when YOUR videos get most views (check analytics)

### Algorithm Specifics

**TikTok Algorithm Loves:**
- ‚è±Ô∏è Watch time (keep them watching to the end)
- üí¨ Comments (respond to everything)
- üîÑ Shares (most important metric)
- ‚èÆÔ∏è Rewatches (loop your videos naturally)
- ‚úÖ Completion rate (% who watch full video)

**TikTok Algorithm Hates:**
- ‚ùå External links
- ‚ùå Watermarks from other platforms
- ‚ùå Asking for engagement ("follow for part 2")
- ‚ùå Posting identical content to other accounts

## üíº LinkedIn Cardinal Rules

### Posts

**DO:**
- ‚úÖ Start with a hook (first 2 lines visible in feed)
- ‚úÖ Tell stories (personal experiences > generic advice)
- ‚úÖ Use line breaks for readability
- ‚úÖ Tag relevant people/companies (1-2 max)
- ‚úÖ Comment on others' posts before posting yours
- ‚úÖ Post 2-3x per week (quality over frequency)
- ‚úÖ Use "Anyone else...?" or "Agree?" to drive comments
- ‚úÖ Respond to comments within 2 hours

**DON'T:**
- ‚ùå Don't use hashtags in post body (LinkedIn deprioritizes them now)
- ‚ùå Don't add links in first comment (kills reach)
- ‚ùå Don't post memes or overly casual content
- ‚ùå Don't tag 10+ people (looks spammy)
- ‚ùå Don't cross-post from Twitter (different tone)

**Best Formats:**
1. Text-only posts (best reach)
2. Single image posts
3. PDF carousels (educational content)
4. Native video (under 3 minutes)

**Timing:**
- Post 7-8am, 12pm, or 5-6pm EST (weekdays)
- Tuesday-Thursday are best days
- Avoid posting on weekends

### LinkedIn Videos

**DO:**
- ‚úÖ Upload directly to LinkedIn (not YouTube links)
- ‚úÖ Add captions (auto-generate, then edit)
- ‚úÖ Square format (1:1) or vertical (4:5)
- ‚úÖ Keep under 3 minutes
- ‚úÖ Professional but conversational tone

**DON'T:**
- ‚ùå Don't post TikTok-style videos (different audience)
- ‚ùå Don't use trending audio (LinkedIn isn't TikTok)

## üê¶ Twitter/X Cardinal Rules

### Tweets

**DO:**
- ‚úÖ Tweet 5-10x per day (high frequency works here)
- ‚úÖ Use thread format for longer content
- ‚úÖ Quote tweet others (drives engagement)
- ‚úÖ Reply to big accounts (visibility)
- ‚úÖ Use 1-2 hashtags max
- ‚úÖ Pin your best tweet
- ‚úÖ Use images/GIFs (1.5x more engagement)

**DON'T:**
- ‚ùå Don't use more than 2 hashtags (looks spammy)
- ‚ùå Don't tweet and ghost (reply to responses)
- ‚ùå Don't delete tweets (looks suspicious)
- ‚ùå Don't post links without context (add your take)

**Timing:**
- Tweet during work hours (9am-5pm EST)
- Weekdays > weekends
- Engage in morning, post in afternoon

## üìå Pinterest Cardinal Rules

### Pins

**DO:**
- ‚úÖ Use vertical images (2:3 ratio, 1000x1500px)
- ‚úÖ Text overlay on images (tell them what it is)
- ‚úÖ Create multiple pins for same content
- ‚úÖ Use keyword-rich descriptions
- ‚úÖ Pin to relevant boards
- ‚úÖ Fresh pins > repins (algorithm prefers new content)

**DON'T:**
- ‚ùå Don't use watermarks with other platform logos
- ‚ùå Don't pin multiple identical pins to same board
- ‚ùå Don't use clickbait titles
- ‚ùå Don't pin broken links

## üé¨ YouTube Cardinal Rules

### Videos

**DO:**
- ‚úÖ Optimize title with keyword (front-loaded)
- ‚úÖ Write detailed description (500+ words)
- ‚úÖ Custom thumbnail (1280x720px, <2MB)
- ‚úÖ Add chapters/timestamps
- ‚úÖ Use 5-7 relevant tags
- ‚úÖ Upload consistently (weekly minimum)
- ‚úÖ Pin a comment asking a question
- ‚úÖ Respond to comments in first 24 hours
- ‚úÖ Add end screens (last 20 seconds)

**DON'T:**
- ‚ùå Don't clickbait (YouTube penalizes this)
- ‚ùå Don't ignore analytics (watch time, CTR, retention)
- ‚ùå Don't skip video description
- ‚ùå Don't use copyrighted music without license
- ‚ùå Don't ask for subs in first 30 seconds (lose viewers)

**YouTube Shorts:**
- Same rules as TikTok/Reels
- Upload as "Shorts" (not regular video)
- Cross-post to TikTok/IG after 24 hours

## üìä Metrics That Actually Matter

### Instagram:
- **Reach** (not followers)
- **Saves** (most important engagement metric)
- **Shares** (second most important)
- **Comments** > Likes
- **Profile visits** (indicates interest)

### TikTok:
- **Watch time** (most important)
- **Completion rate** (did they watch to end?)
- **Shares** (algorithm's favorite metric)
- **Comments**
- **Followers** (least important)

### LinkedIn:
- **Engagement rate** (comments + reactions / impressions)
- **Profile views** (from your posts)
- **Connection requests** (indicates authority)
- **Post impressions** (reach)

### YouTube:
- **Watch time** (most important)
- **Click-through rate (CTR)** on thumbnails
- **Audience retention** (keep them watching)
- **Subscribers** from video
- **Average view duration**

## üö´ Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **‚ùå Posting and ghosting** - Respond to comments!
2. **‚ùå Using the same caption everywhere** - Adapt for each platform
3. **‚ùå Ignoring analytics** - Data tells you what works
4. **‚ùå Inconsistent posting** - Pick a schedule and stick to it
5. **‚ùå Too salesy** - 80% value, 20% promotion
6. **‚ùå Not engaging with others** - Social media is social!
7. **‚ùå Chasing every trend** - Only do trends relevant to your niche
8. **‚ùå Forgetting to add CTA** - Tell people what to do next
9. **‚ùå Not testing** - Try different formats, analyze results
10. **‚ùå Comparing to others** - Focus on YOUR growth

## üì± Quick Pre-Post Checklist

Before hitting "Post," ask yourself:

- [ ] Is this native to the platform? (not a cross-post)
- [ ] Is the format optimized? (vertical for Reels, professional for LinkedIn)
- [ ] Did I add captions/subtitles? (80% watch without sound)
- [ ] Is the hook strong? (first 3 seconds grab attention)
- [ ] Did I add a call-to-action? (comment, share, save)
- [ ] Is the quality high? (no blurry images, clear audio)
- [ ] Am I posting at the right time? (check your analytics)
- [ ] Can I engage for the next 30-60 minutes? (critical engagement window)
- [ ] Did I proofread? (typos hurt credibility)
- [ ] Does this provide value? (entertain, educate, or inspire)
