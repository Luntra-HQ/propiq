# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 10, 2025**

PropIQ ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information
When you register for PropIQ, we collect:
- Email address
- Full name (optional)
- Password (encrypted)
- Payment information (processed by Stripe - we do not store credit card numbers)

### 1.2 Usage Data
We automatically collect:
- Property addresses you analyze
- Analysis results and history
- Support chat conversations
- Usage statistics (number of analyses performed)
- Login activity and timestamps
- IP address and browser information

### 1.3 Payment Information
- Subscription tier and status
- Payment history (via Stripe)
- Billing information
- We use Stripe for payment processing and do not directly handle credit card information

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the PropIQ service
- Process property analyses and generate investment insights
- Manage your subscription and billing
- Send service-related emails (welcome, billing, important updates)
- Respond to support inquiries
- Improve our service and develop new features
- Comply with legal obligations
- Prevent fraud and unauthorized use

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Union, we process your data based on:
- **Contract Performance**: Processing necessary to provide the service you signed up for
- **Legitimate Interests**: Improving our service, fraud prevention, analytics
- **Legal Obligation**: Compliance with tax, accounting, and legal requirements
- **Consent**: Marketing communications (you can opt-out anytime)

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 Service Providers
We share data with trusted third parties:
- **Stripe**: Payment processing
- **SendGrid**: Transactional emails
- **Sentry**: Error monitoring and debugging
- **Supabase**: Database hosting

### 4.2 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by law or to:
- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect our rights and safety
- Investigate fraud or security issues
- Enforce our Terms of Service

### 4.3 Business Transfers
If PropIQ is acquired or merged, user data may be transferred to the new entity.

### 4.4 No Selling of Data
We **never** sell your personal information to third parties.

## 5. Your Privacy Rights

### 5.1 All Users
- **Access**: View your personal data
- **Correction**: Update incorrect information
- **Deletion**: Request account deletion
- **Export**: Download your data
- **Opt-out**: Unsubscribe from marketing emails

### 5.2 GDPR Rights (EU Users)
- **Right to Access**: Request a copy of your data (Article 15)
- **Right to Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data (Article 16)
- **Right to Erasure**: Request deletion of your data (Article 17)
- **Right to Restrict Processing**: Limit how we use your data (Article 18)
- **Right to Data Portability**: Receive your data in a portable format (Article 20)
- **Right to Object**: Object to processing based on legitimate interests (Article 21)
- **Right to Withdraw Consent**: Withdraw consent at any time

### 5.3 CCPA Rights (California Users)
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
- Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
- Right to deletion of personal information
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your data:
- **Active Accounts**: As long as your account is active
- **Inactive Accounts**: 2 years after last login, then deleted
- **Deleted Accounts**: 30-day grace period, then permanently deleted
- **Legal Requirements**: Longer if required by law (e.g., tax records: 7 years)

## 8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **Encryption**: All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
- **Authentication**: JWT tokens with 7-day expiration
- **Password Security**: Bcrypt hashing with salt
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access, service-level security
- **Monitoring**: Real-time error monitoring and alerts
- **Backups**: Regular encrypted backups

## 9. Cookies and Tracking

### 9.1 Essential Cookies
- **Authentication token**: Required for login
- **Session management**: Required for using the service

### 9.2 Analytics (Optional)
- We may use analytics to improve the service
- You can disable analytics cookies in Settings

### 9.3 No Third-Party Advertising
We do not use advertising cookies or track you for advertising purposes.

## 10. International Data Transfers

PropIQ is based in the United States. By using our service, you consent to your data being transferred to and processed in the US.

For EU users, we ensure adequate protection through:
- Standard Contractual Clauses with third-party processors
- Compliance with GDPR requirements
- Data processing agreements with all subprocessors

## 11. Children's Privacy

PropIQ is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

## 12. California "Do Not Track" Disclosure

PropIQ does not track users across third-party websites. We do not respond to Do Not Track signals because we don't track users for advertising.
