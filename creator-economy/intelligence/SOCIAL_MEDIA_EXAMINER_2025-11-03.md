### 1. Instagram Post Timing Fix - **CRITICAL**
**Problem:** Sharing to Stories immediately after posting kills feed reach
**Solution:** Post sequence strategy

**The Right Way:**
1. Publish post to feed FIRST
2. **WAIT 2-3 hours** (let algorithm push to Explore/Recommendations)
3. THEN share to Stories

**Why It Works:**
- Instagram algorithm prioritizes ONE content type at a time
- Immediate Story share signals "this is just a Story"
- Waiting allows post to gain momentum in feed/Explore
- Result: Extended post life + higher reach

**PropIQ Application:**
- Tuesday 7:00 AM: Post LinkedIn-style content to Instagram feed
- Tuesday 10:00 AM: Share to Stories with "New post up!" sticker
- Measure: Compare reach with old method (immediate share)

**Source:** Jenn Herman (Instagram expert featured in newsletter)
